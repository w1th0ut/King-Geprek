About King Geprek

The geprek chicken business is a development of the fried chicken business which we usually know as fried chicken. However, this fried chicken is more adapted to the tastes of most Indonesian people who like spicy, so it becomes a geprek chicken business opportunity. Usually geprek chicken can be ordered with various levels of spicy. we are interested in making this business because smashed fried chicken is now a dish that we can find anywhere.
